Our Vision

PowerMakerAI is more than a tool — it’s your AI pair programmer for Dynamics 365. Whether you're a solo consultant or an enterprise team, PowerMakerAI helps you move faster, debug smarter, and ship confidently. 

PowerMakerAI is built with the vision of simplifying Dynamics 365 customizations using AI. The MVP is already live and evolving rapidly based on developer and consultant feedback.  

Current Features

AI Entity Builder withVisualizer

Transform natural language into structured CRM schema effortlessly. 

What it does

PowerMakerAI turns plain English into structured CRM schemas instantly. For example, saying “Create a Contact entity with name, email, phone, and a custom status field” generates a complete entity setup with proper attribute types and configuration

Visualizer Highlights 

  • Interactive drag-and-drop schema builder
  • Visual hierarchy of entities and fields
  • Auto-detection of attribute types based on intent
  • Real-time CRM entity preview

Why it matters

It saves developers and consultants time by replacing complex Dynamics 365 customization steps with a simple, conversational and visual approach to entity creation.

Plugin Trace Log Analyzer

Diagnose plugin errors without digging through endless logs. 

What it does

PowerMakerAI simplifies diagnosing plugin errors by analyzing raw PluginTraceLog content or uploaded trace files. It highlights failed plugins, summarizes root causes, and offers likely solutions.

Visualizer Highlights 

  • Parses complex, multi-level logs
  • Identifies failed steps, plugins, and correlation IDs
  • Suggests fixes and highlights affected CRM areas
  • Supports both synchronous and asynchronous logs

Why it matters

It drastically reduces the time CRM developers spend analyzing logs, delivering quick, accurate insights and minimizing trial-and-error debugging.

Advanced AI ChatBot with Live CRM Interaction

Chat with your CRM like you chat with a colleague — only smarter. 

What it does

Describe your entity in plain English – “Create a Contact entity with name, email, phone, and a custom status field” – and PowerMakerAI will generate a full entity configuration, complete with attribute types, requirements, and schema suggestions. 

Visualizer Highlights 

  • Interactive schema builder with drag-and-drop support
  • Clear visual hierarchy of entities and attributes
  • Auto-detects attribute type based on name & intent
  • Instant preview of how your entity will look inside CRM 

Why it matters

Developers and consultants often lose time navigating through multiple Dynamics 365 customizations screens. PowerMakerAI streamlines this by making entity creation as easy as talking to a colleague — and visualizing the output in real time.

Key Features

Natural Language Entity Builder

Create entities and attributes by simply describing them — no need to click through endless setup forms. 

Smart Plugin Trace Log Analyzer

Paste your logs and get instant summaries, root cause explanations, and fix suggestions. 

Code-Free Attribute Designer

Define field types, requirements, and relationships without writing a single line of code.  

Conversational AI Assistant

Chat with PowerMakerAI to create, update, or query CRM data — with context-aware, multi-turn interactions.  

Auto-Generated Plugin & JS Snippets

Turn requirements into ready-to-deploy C# plugin or JavaScript code blocks, tailored for your CRM instance.  

One-Click CRM Export

Generate solution-ready JSON or metadata snippets and push to your CRM with a single action. 

Built-in CRM Schema Visualizer

View entity relationships, attributes, and data types in an interactive, user-friendly diagram. 

Enterprise-Ready Security

Your data is safe — encrypted handling, API token-based access, and deployment-ready for secure environments.  

Scalable & Extendabler

Built to grow with your team. Add more models, integrate deeper with CRM APIs, or plug into CI/CD pipelines.
